                    Our Commitment to Child Safety                

               

DevPark's zero-tolerance policy against CSAE.

           
       
               
           
               

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

               

                  At DevPark, we are dedicated to providing a safe and secure environment for all users, particularly children. We have a **zero-tolerance policy** against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and are committed to combating this crime through a combination of proactive measures, robust reporting systems, and collaboration with law enforcement.                

               

Our Core Principles

               
                       
  • **Zero Tolerance:** Any content, behavior, or activity related to CSAE is strictly forbidden. This includes the creation, sharing, distribution, or promotion of such material.
    •                    
  • **Immediate Action:** Upon detection or report of CSAE, we will take swift and decisive action to remove the content and suspend the user's account.
    •                    
  • **Mandatory Reporting:** We are legally and ethically obligated to report all instances of CSAE to the appropriate authorities, including the **National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)** and law enforcement.
    •                
               

How We Address CSAE

               
                       
  • **Detection and Prevention:** We utilize a combination of automated and human review systems to actively scan for and identify known CSAE content. We employ industry-standard content hashes and other technological solutions to prevent the re-upload of illegal material.
    •                    
  • **User Reporting:** We encourage our community to be our partners in safety. You can report any suspicious content or behavior by using our **"Report an Issue"** link in the footer or by emailing us at devpark.contact@gmail.com. All reports are handled with the highest level of urgency and confidentiality.
    •                    
  • **Collaboration with Law Enforcement:** We work closely with law enforcement agencies globally and will provide all necessary information to aid in their investigations of individuals involved in CSAE.
    •                
               

Enforcement and Penalties

               

                  Any user who violates this policy will face immediate and permanent termination of their account. Their information will be retained and provided to law enforcement for criminal investigation.                

               

                  By using DevPark, you agree to these standards. We are unwavering in our commitment to creating a safe and positive online space for everyone.
