DevPark's zero-tolerance policy against CSAE.

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At DevPark, we are dedicated to providing a safe and secure environment for all users, particularly children. We have a zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and are committed to combating this crime through a combination of proactive measures, robust reporting systems, and collaboration with law enforcement.

Our Core Principles

  • Zero Tolerance: Any content, behavior, or activity related to CSAE is strictly forbidden.
  • Immediate Action: We will take swift and decisive action to remove content and suspend accounts.
  • Mandatory Reporting: We report all instances of CSAE to NCMEC and law enforcement.

How We Address CSAE

  • Detection and Prevention: We use automated and manual systems to detect CSAE content.
  • User Reporting: Report issues via footer or email devpark.contact@gmail.com.
  • Collaboration with Law Enforcement: We assist authorities in investigations.

Enforcement and Penalties

Violators will face immediate and permanent termination. Their information will be shared with law enforcement.

By using DevPark, you agree to these standards. We are committed to a safe and positive online space for all.
