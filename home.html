Privacy Policy

At DevPark, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website devpark.com and use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the site, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the website (such as posting messages, creating polls, attending hackathons), or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the Website, the choices you make, and the products and features you use. The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • Name and contact data (email address, optional profile links)
  • Credentials (passwords, password hints, and similar security information used for authentication and account access)
  • Content you provide (ideas, write-ups, poll responses, project details)
  • Usage data (information about how you interact with ideas, projects, and community features)

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our Website for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you, with your consent, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations.

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To enable community features like idea sharing, write-ups, polls, and hackathons.
  • To facilitate direct collaboration between users on posted ideas, including potential investment discussions.
  • To send you transactional and optional marketing/promotional communications.
  • To send administrative information to you.
  • To protect our Services and ensure platform integrity (e.g., through reporting systems).
  • To respond to user inquiries/offer support to users.
  • To request feedback and improve our services.
  • To enable us to manage user accounts.

For more detailed information on how we use your data, data sharing, your rights, and more, please review the full policy available here.
